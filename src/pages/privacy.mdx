### RIGHTS ACCORDING TO LEGAL DATA PROTECTION REGULATIONS

You have the rights listed below under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You may assert your rights at any time free of charge.

**Right of Access by the Data Subject**: You have the right to receive information regarding the processing of your personal data.

**Right to Rectification**: You have the right to demand that we correct any personal data that
is incorrect and/or incomplete.

**Right to Erasure**: You have the right, in the event that the requirements specified in Art. 17 of the GDPR have been met, to demand the deletion of your data. For example, you may demand the deletion of your data if that data is no longer required for the purposes for which is was collected.

**Right to Restriction of Processing**: You have the right to demand the restriction of the processing of your data if the requirements specified in Art. 18 of the GDPR have been fulfilled.

**Right to Object**: You have the right to object to the processing of your data for the purposes of direct advertising.

**Right to Data Portability**: You have the right to receive your data in a structured, commonplace and machine-readable format and to request the transfer of this data to you or a third party.

**Right of Revocation**: You have the right to withdraw your consent for processing your data at any time free of charge.

**Right to Complain**: You have the right to complain to a government supervisory authority regarding our processing of your data.
